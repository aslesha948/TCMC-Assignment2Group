The Townsville Concert Band

The Townsville Concert Band are currently looking for musician members.

This is a local community band who meet once a week for rehearsals, and who play gigs around town throughout the year. The band plays a range of different styles of concert band music, ranging from classical to musical theatre and modern. All levels and ages are welcome.
